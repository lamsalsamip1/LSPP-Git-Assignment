Story Title: The Quest for Harmony: A Tale of Friendship and Magic

In a faraway land, there was a young orphan named Harry Potter who lived with his cruel aunt, uncle, and cousin. They treated him poorly and never told him about his magical heritage.

Discovery of Hogwarts: On his eleventh birthday, Harry received a letter informing him that he was accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Hagrid, the gamekeeper of Hogwarts, came to deliver the letter and take Harry to the magical world.
